## Description (4000 characters max)

```
Totus Secure Notes keeps your notes encrypted on your iPhone or iPad with AES-256. No cloud account required — your data stays on your device.

SECURE NOTE-TAKING
• Create markdown notes with flags, local reminders, follow-up tracking, and task digest
• Optional on-device AI summary atop open follow-ups (Pro Lifetime)
• Note Assist (Pro Lifetime): bulletize, shorten, expand, or summarize on-device
• Contextual Assist chips on Notes, Templates, and Trips
• Master password plus optional Face ID / Touch ID unlock
• Show or hide password while entering credentials
• Light, dark, and system appearance
• Encrypted photo, audio, and video attachments with gallery scrub and secure delete
• In-app attachment viewer with screenshot blocking
• Export and import encrypted backup files

CLINICAL-STYLE TEMPLATES
• Postpartum nursing note template — manual weight fields for copy/paste
• Built-in briefcase templates: home visit, wound care, psychosocial, discharge, intake
• Template library — curated public templates (metadata only); import locally
• Copy for Plexia / EMR: plain Label: value export from filled forms
• Template Studio (Pro Lifetime): create custom templates from pasted forms
• Template AI (Pro Lifetime): on-device AI field suggestions — review before save
• Totus Assist hub (Settings → Totus Assist): model status, capabilities, troubleshooting
• Patient address field with option to add the visit to today's trip plan

TRIP & MILEAGE PLANNER
• Plan up to 50 stops for your visit day
• GPS trip recording logs actual kilometers driven
• Open your route in Apple Maps or Google Maps — multi-stop, no API keys
• Trip Planner Pro (Pro Lifetime): driving route distance and in-app map preview

DESKTOP WEB VIEWER
• Settings → Sync to desktop: export .totus bundle, transfer file, open https://totus--notes.web.app/vault (read-only browser decryption)
• Decryption happens in your browser only — no cloud upload

PRIVACY & CONTROL
• Notes and trip data encrypted locally — not uploaded to Totus Life servers
• Template AI and Note Assist run on-device only
• Auto-lock, optional screenshot blocking, clipboard timeout for sensitive copies
• No account required

FREE & PRO
Free to download. Pro Monthly removes ads. Pro Lifetime unlocks Trip Planner Pro, Template Studio, Template AI, Note Assist, and all premium features.

IMPORTANT
This app is a productivity tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. On-device AI is productivity assistance only — not clinical decision support. You are responsible for the accuracy of your notes and compliance with workplace and privacy regulations. We do not claim HIPAA certification or FDA/Health Canada approval.

Questions? totuslife7@gmail.com
```
