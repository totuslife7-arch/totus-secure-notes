Summary

Totus Secure Notes does not require a cloud account. Your notes are stored on your device. There is no Totus Life server holding your vault. This page satisfies Google Play account and data deletion requirements for apps without cloud accounts.

1. Delete all app data on your device

Android:

1. Open Settings → Apps → Totus Secure Notes

2. Tap Storage & cache → Clear storage (or Clear data)

3. Confirm

Alternatively, uninstall the app to remove local data.

iOS:

1. Delete the app from your home screen

2. Or Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Totus Secure Notes → Delete App

This removes the encrypted vault, trip data, voice memo attachments, downloaded Template AI model (if any), settings, and local entitlements cache.

2. Delete exported backup files

If you exported an encrypted vault (.enc file) to Files, email, or cloud storage, you must delete those files separately. We cannot delete files on your device or third-party storage.

3. In-app purchases and subscriptions

Purchase records are held by Google Play or Apple App Store, not Totus Life.

  • Cancel subscriptions: Play Store → Payments & subscriptions, or App Store → Subscriptions
  • Refund requests: Use the store’s refund process
  • License testing: Test purchases may be revoked when removed from license testers

Deleting app data does not automatically refund purchases.

4. Firebase analytics and crash data

Anonymous analytics and crash reports sent to Google Firebase are governed by Google’s data policies. To limit future collection, uninstall the app. To request deletion of data Google holds, use Google’s privacy tools.

We do not maintain a separate Totus Life database of analytics tied to your identity.

5. Request assistance

If you cannot delete data yourself, email totuslife7@gmail.com with:

  • Device type (Android/iOS)
  • App version
  • What you want deleted

We will provide step-by-step guidance. We cannot remotely wipe your device.

6. No cloud account

Because there is no Totus Secure Notes cloud login, there is no remote “account deletion” on our servers. Local deletion (Section 1) is the complete removal method for note content.
