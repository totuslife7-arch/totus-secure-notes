## Content

The app contains:

- Encrypted note-taking tools  
- Optional **clinical-style templates** (postpartum nursing notes) — **not medical advice**  
- Optional **location** for mileage logging  
- Optional **advertisements** (free tier)  
- **In-app purchases**  

There is no user-generated public content, social features, or chat.

---

## Children’s privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA where applicable). If you believe a child provided information, contact **totuslife7@gmail.com** and we will assist with deletion steps on the device.

---

## Ads and children

We do **not** serve ads in a child-directed mode. The app is not listed in Designed for Families. AdMob is configured for a general/adult productivity audience.
